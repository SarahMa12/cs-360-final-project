Mental Health in Tech

By Sarah Ma

Project Description

This project explores the effect of workplace culture on mental health within the tech industry. Using the OSMI Mental Health in Tech Survey dataset, this interactive dashboard allows users to visualize how factors like company size, accessibility to support systems, and demographics impact employee mental health. This project will have a total of 4 visualizations, including a geographic mapping, parallel coordinates, diverging bar chart, and a sunburst diagram.

Geographic Support Distribution

Objective: How do personal demographics and location relate to mental health support in tech?

This choropleth map visualizes the average Support Score across the United States.

Support Accessibility by Company Size

Objective: How much mental health support is there in the tech industry, specifically comparing small vs. big companies?

This diverging bar chart shows specific support features, like benefits and anonymity, to show where support gaps exists.

Prevalence of Mental Health Issues

Objective: How prevalent are mental health issues among people working in tech?

The inner ring shows the percentage of the industry seeking treatment, while the outer ring reveals how that prevalence shifts across company sizes.
